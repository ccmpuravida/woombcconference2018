What is the conference?

In the International Conference there are important speaches
by researchers of different cientific and social areas.

This 2018 the International Conference of the Billings Ovulation Method® will be hosted in San José, Costa Rica. This is the first time the World Conference will be held in America.

The conference will have two days of research speaches in fields related to fertility, teaching of the BOM, bioethic considerations and related subjects.

As usual, the conference will be followed by four days of basic and extension teacher training programs taught by the directors of WOOMB International.

Inspiring Speaches

If you don't know what is the Billings Ovulation Method® you will be able to learn the basics and even receive tranings.
Get up to speed with the latest research done.

Workshops

  • Basic Teacher Training Program
  • Extension Program

Conference Venue

On April 27th and 28th the conference will be held at Colegio de Abogados de Costa Rica.

Trainings Venue
