San José, Costa Rica.

Learn More

What is the conference?

Important presentations by researchers from different scientific
and social areas will be offered at the conference..

The 2018 International Conference of the Billings Ovulation Method® will be held in San José, Costa Rica. This is the first time the International Conference will be in America.

The conference will consist of two days, offering a variety of presentations on topis related to fertility, bioethical considerations on teaching the Billings Ovulation Method®, and othere relevant subjects.

As usual, the conference will be followed by four days of basic and extension teacher training programs taught by the directors of WOOMB International.

Inspiring Presentations

This Conference will give you information about the Billings Ovulation Method®, its scientific basis and some of the current research. By attending the Teacher Training Programs following the Conference you will gain the skills to share this knowledge with others.

Get the latest updates on the current research being done.

Workshops

The following Teacher Training Courses will be held beginning Sunday, April 29th afternoon and continuing until Wednesday, May 2nd:

  • Basic/Upskilling Teacher Training Program
  • Extension Program for experienced teachers

Conference Venue

On Friday, April 27th and Saturday, April 28th the conference will be held at Colegio de Abogados de Costa Rica.

Teacher Training Venue

From Sunday, April 29th to Wednesday, May 2nd, trainings will be held at the Pastoral House María Inés Teresa Arias of the Sisters of Saint Clare (Misioneras Clarisas del Santísimo Sacramento).
