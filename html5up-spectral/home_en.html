What is the conference?

Important presentations by researchers of different scientific
and social areas will be offered at the conference..

The 2018 International Conference of the Billings Ovulation Method® will be held in San José, Costa Rica. This is the first time the International Conference will be in America.

The conference will consist of two days offering a variety of presentations on topis related to fertility, bioethics considerations on teaching the Billings Ovulation Method®, and othere relevant subjects.

As usual, the conference will be followed by four days of basic and extension teacher training programs taught by the directors of WOOMB International.

Inspiring Presentations

If you don't know what the Billings Ovulation Method® is, at the conference you will learn the basics and if interested receive traning.
Get the latest updates on the current research being done.

Workshops

  • Basic Teacher Training Program
  • Extension Program

Conference Venue

Trainings Venue
